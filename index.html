The Album

Between punk instinct
& fragile introspection

An introspective album wandering fluidly between alternative pop, ambient and experimental soundscapes — creating a poetic world both intimate and unbound, unclean and forgiving.

Conceived as a cross-art experience alongside its accompanying film, Fear Of The Beauty Parlour carries a raw punk spirit through a truly subconscious expression of songwriting and production.
